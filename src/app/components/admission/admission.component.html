Admissions are Open for 2019-20

Registration:
  • Registrations for Pre-School (Nursery) would be in the third week of November every year. Registration forms can be obtained from the school office.
  • The norms for admission process are followed carefully to provide equal opportunity to all applicants and to maintain transparency in the admission procedure.
Age limit:
  • The child should complete the age of 3 years as on 31st of March.
Documents Required:
  • Two Passport Size photograph of the child.
  • Birth Certificate issued by municipal corporation or other competent authority.
  • Address proof, SAMAGRA ID, Aadhar Card, Caste Certificate and Copy of Bank Pass book of child if eligible for government scholarship.
  • Parents are requested to produce the attested photocopies of all the relevant documents at the time of admissions and submit the same along with the school registration form completed in all respect at the school office.
General:
  • For further assistance on admission please feel free to contact the admission in charge @ 07582-237677, 260521.
  • Mail your queries to info@parasvidyavihar.org or parasvidyavihar@yahoo.co.in for admission to higher classes.
  • On confirmation of admission from the school, fees are to be deposited within 03 working days, failing which the seat will be offered to the next candidate on the waiting list.
